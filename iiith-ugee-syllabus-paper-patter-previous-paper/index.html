IIITH UGEE 2021 - Highlights

IIITH UGEE 2021 – Highlights : The UGEE will have two parts: section 1 SUPR (Subject Proficiency Test) and section 2 REAP (Research Aptitude Test) All the Questions of IIITH UGEE 2021 will be objective in nature. The examination will be conducted in computer-based mode at various centers across the city
SUPR Subject Proficiency Test: The syllabus is CBSE X1 and XII standard Physics/Mathematics/Chemistry
REAP Research aptitude test  : The syllabus is also CBSE X1 and XII standard Physics/Mathematics/Chemistry. Get REAP Complete Guide Click Here
REAP section – The test will be on critical and creative thinking. Get REAP Complete Guide Click Here

             IIITH Undergraduate Entrance Examination (UGEE) 2021 will be conducted by the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT ) Hyderabad followed by an interview. IIIT UGEE 2021 Admit card will be made available for download and the UGEE exam would be conducted in 1st Week of June 2021.
