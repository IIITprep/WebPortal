1.4.7 IIITH UGEE 2021 - Highlights

             IIITH Undergraduate Entrance Examination (UGEE) 2021 will be conducted by the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT ) Hyderabad followed by an interview. IIIT UGEE application form was released on 1st Week of March 2021 and the last date to submit the form was 1st Week of April. IIIT UGEE 2021 Admit card will be made available for download and the UGEE exam would be conducted in 1st Week of June 2021.

IIITH UGEE 2021 Highlights : The UGEE will have two parts: section 1 SUPR (Subject Proficiency Test) and section 2 REAP (Research Aptitude Test) All the Questions of IIITH UGEE 2021 will be objective in nature. The examination will be conducted in computer-based mode at various centers across the city
